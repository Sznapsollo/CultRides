About Us

We are team of petrol-heads and car geeks. Cultrides.com was created to reveal which make and model of all production cars has the greatest following, which one is the coolest or simply most desired.

What ride do you value most and why? For each car you can post links to projects or just some really interesting stuff related to given model. We will share of them on our facebook and Gooogle+ page.

This ranking is not created by car magazines. It is created by our visitors including You. We are grateful for your part in the ranking. Thanks!

If a car that you want to vote for is not in our database then let us know by going to "Add Cult Ride" tab and leaving a comment.
